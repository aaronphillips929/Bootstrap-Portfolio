About Me

Aaron Phillips was born and raised in Austin, TX. He attended St. Stephen's Episcopal School from grades 6 through 12. After graduating from St. Stephen's with honors, Aaron moved to San Diego to attend University of San Diego. He graduated USD in 2011 with a degree in Business Administration.

Upon graduating, Aaron moved back to Austin to work in the family business, Centex Machining. Located in Round Rock, TX, Centex Machining is a contract manufacturer for the medical device industry. Centex primarily manufactures hip, knee, and spinal implants and surgical instruments. Aaron became a buyer/planner for Centex Machining where he excelled at contract negotiation, lean six sigma manufacturing standards, and inventory management.

In 2016, Aaron was promoted to Vice President of Operations at Centex Machining. In his tenure as VP of Ops, Aaron led efforts to maximize EBITDA in order to position the company for sale. Centex Machining was sold in July of 2019 to an independent manufacturer.
