About Me

My name is Aaron Phillips and I eat, breath, and sleep code. I see web development as a platform to build the future in a way that seemlessly enhances the experience between computers and users. I aspire to develop web apps that are effortless to use but make an impact on your brand's user engagement.

I work with brands to develop web and mobile apps that users get addicted to. I use web development as a tool to break down the barrier between brands and their customers. When done right, the apps I develop are a trojan horse to get inside the minds of your customers. I utilize innovative ideas and methods to add a personable touch to your brand and its digital assets.

We've just barely scratched the surface when it comes to what web and mobile apps can do for brands. Let's create something new and groundbreaking together. Let's raise the standard of what brands can expect from developers. Let's build something new, innovative, and appealing together.
